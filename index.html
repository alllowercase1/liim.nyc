About Liim Lasalle

Since the release of his 2022 debut single "Pupils" (with Opraah), Liim has quickly become one of New York's most promising new artists. Drawing on bespoke, jazz-infused production, jagged rhythms, ghostly soul fragments, and freeform lyrical flows, the 22-year-old Harlem-raised artist's genre-blending approach has resulted in a sound that bends structure and mood, fusing hip-hop, soul, and alternative soundscapes that reflect his New York City upbringing.

Having recently modeled for Gucci and Supreme, Liim has also gained the recognition of Tyler, The Creator for tracks like "Petty Pete", "Edward 40 Handz", and "Older Than Me", rapidly establishing himself within the NYC creative community and beyond.
